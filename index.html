Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and booking process.

How do I book a flight or hotel? ⌄
Simply fill out our booking form with your travel details. Our team will get back to you within 24 hours with the best options available for your needs.
What payment methods do you accept? ⌄
We accept credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), bank transfers, GCash, Maya, and online banking. All transactions are processed securely through trusted payment gateways. Visit our Payment Options page for more details.
Can I cancel or modify my booking? ⌄
Yes, cancellations and modifications are possible depending on the supplier's terms. Contact us immediately if you need to make changes, and we'll assist you. Cancellation policies vary by service, so we'll provide details when confirming your booking.
How long does visa processing take? ⌄
Processing time varies by country, typically ranging from 5-15 business days. We process tourist visas only. We'll provide you with an estimated timeline once we review your documents.
Do you offer group discounts? ⌄
Yes! We offer special rates for group bookings. Contact our team to discuss group packages and pricing options. We can customize tours and travel arrangements for groups of any size.
What is your customer support availability? ⌄
We're available 24/7 to assist you. You can reach us via email, phone, Facebook Messenger, or our contact form. We typically reply to emails within 24 hours and Messenger messages within a few minutes.
Do you offer travel insurance? ⌄
Travel insurance is available as an add-on to most packages. Contact us to discuss insurance options and coverage details for your trip.
What documents do I need for a tourist visa? ⌄
Required documents vary by destination and your nationality. Common requirements include a valid passport, proof of funds, travel itinerary, and accommodation booking. Contact us for specific requirements for your destination.
